India

India's culture is among the world's oldest; civilization in India began about 4,500 years ago.Indians have made significant advances in architecture, mathematics and medicine. Today, India has a population of 1.2 billion people,making it the second most populous nation after China. India is the 7th largest country by area.

There are 29 states and 7 Union territories in the country. Hindi is the official language of the government.Though many people speak Hindi in India, 59 percent of India residents speak something other than Hindi.

About 84 percent of the population identifies as Hindu.About 13 percent of Indians are Muslim, making it one of the largest Islamic nations in the world. Christians and Sikhs make up a small percentage of the population, and there are even fewer Buddhists and Jains. The highest mountain in India is Kanchenjunga, standing at 8,598m (28,209 ft), which it shares with Nepal. Kanchenjunga is the third highest mountain in the world.
